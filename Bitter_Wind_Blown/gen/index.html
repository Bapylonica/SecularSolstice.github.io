Bitter Wind Blown

By Raymond Arnold

A lullaby for a child that is shivering in the cold

This song takes on the "winter" concept most directly: the cold and darkness and danger for the unprepared.

In some places, it is referred to as "Bitter Wind Lullaby" to distinguish it from the later Bitter Wind March
